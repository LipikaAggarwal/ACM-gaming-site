Game Jam 2023

An intensive 48-hour game development competition where teams of developers, artists, and designers come together to create unique games from scratch. Participants showcase their creativity and skills while working under pressure to meet a specific theme.

Coding Marathon

A hands-on workshop introducing participants to game development fundamentals. Covering topics such as game design principles, programming basics using Unity or Unreal Engine, and creating a simple game prototype by the end of the session.

eSports Tournament

A thrilling tournament featuring popular competitive games like League of Legends, Dota 2, or Valorant. Teams compete for glory and prizes while the event fosters a spirited community atmosphere. Spectators can cheer on their favorite teams.
